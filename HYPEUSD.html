Dati finanziari per: Hyperliquid (HYPEUSD)

Hyperliquid is a decentralized perpetual exchange (DEX) built on its own specialized L1 blockchain, known for its high performance and low-latency trading experience. Unlike many other DEXs, Hyperliquid operates with a fully on-chain order book, allowing users to trade a wide variety of assets with deep liquidity and minimal slippage. The platform is designed to mimic the efficiency of centralized exchanges while maintaining the security and transparency of a decentralized system. Its native token, HYPE, plays a central role in the ecosystem, facilitating governance, securing the network through staking, and driving the protocol's long-term growth as it expands into a broader financial infrastructure.
