Dati finanziari per: Emirates NBD Bank PJSC (EMIRATESNBD.AE)

Emirates NBD Bank PJSC is a leading banking group in the Middle East and a major player in the Dubai financial landscape. The group operates across various business units including Retail Banking and Wealth Management, Corporate and Institutional Banking, and Global Markets and Treasury. Emirates NBD has a significant international presence, particularly in Turkey (through DenizBank), Egypt, and India. The bank is a pioneer in digital banking within the UAE, having launched Liv., the region's first lifestyle digital bank for millennials. The group is currently focusing on artificial intelligence and cloud-based infrastructure to drive operational efficiency and personalized customer experiences. Its headquarters are in Dubai, UAE.
