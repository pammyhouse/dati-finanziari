Dati finanziari per: Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJ30)

The Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJ30) is one of the oldest and most widely recognized stock market indices in the United States, consisting of 30 large, publicly traded blue-chip companies spanning diverse sectors such as technology, finance, healthcare, and industrials. It is a price-weighted index that serves as a barometer for the overall health of the U.S. stock market and economy, tracking the performance of leading companies like Boeing, Coca-Cola, Apple, and Goldman Sachs. The DJI is frequently cited in financial news and analysis, and it influences investor sentiment and portfolio strategies worldwide. Despite its relatively small number of components compared to broader indices, the Dow plays a significant role in market benchmarking and investment products.
