Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI South Korea ETF (EWY)

EWY is the iShares MSCI South Korea ETF, which tracks an index of large- and mid-cap South Korean equities. The fund offers targeted exposure to one of the most dynamic and technologically advanced economies in Asia, with significant weightings in the information technology, consumer discretionary, and financials sectors. By investing in EWY, market participants gain access to global leaders in semiconductors, electronics, and automotive manufacturing listed on the Korea Exchange. The ETF's performance is driven by global demand for high-tech components, domestic fiscal policies, regional geopolitical stability, and the overall health of international trade. It serves as a primary vehicle for investors seeking to capture the growth potential and industrial innovation of the South Korean market within a globally diversified portfolio.
