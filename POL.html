Dati finanziari per: Polygon (POL)

Polygon (formerly MATIC) is a leading Layer-2 scaling solution for Ethereum, designed to provide faster and cheaper transactions through an ecosystem of interconnected ZK-powered chains. In 2026, the network completed its major transition to the POL token, which serves as a hyper-productive utility token for staking, security, and governance across the entire AggLayer (Aggregation Layer). Polygon's architecture, featuring the zkEVM and the Lisovo hardfork improvements, aims to unify liquidity across decentralized networks while supporting massive throughput for enterprise-grade applications. As a cornerstone of the Web3 infrastructure, Polygon continues to facilitate the growth of DeFi, gaming, and NFT sectors by addressing Ethereum's scalability challenges.
