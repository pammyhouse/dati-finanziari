Dati finanziari per: Dai (DAIUSD)

Dai (DAI) is a decentralized, unbiased, collateral-backed stablecoin soft-pegged to the US Dollar. Managed by the MakerDAO decentralized autonomous organization, Dai is held in cryptocurrency wallets or within platforms and is supported on Ethereum and other popular blockchains. Unlike centralized stablecoins, Dai is not issued by a single private company but is instead minted by users who deposit collateral assets into smart contracts. In 2026, Dai remains a fundamental pillar of the DeFi ecosystem, known for its transparency and censorship resistance. Its value is maintained through a dynamic system of collateralized debt positions (CDPs), autonomous feedback mechanisms, and incentivized external actors, ensuring it stays close to $1 even during high market volatility.
