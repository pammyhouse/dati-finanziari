Dati finanziari per: First Abu Dhabi Bank P.J.S.C. (FAB.AD)

First Abu Dhabi Bank P.J.S.C. (FAB) is the largest bank in the United Arab Emirates and one of the world's largest and safest financial institutions. Resulting from the merger of National Bank of Abu Dhabi and First Gulf Bank, FAB provides a comprehensive range of solutions through its Investment Banking and Corporate and Personal Banking franchises. The bank is a key financial engine for the Abu Dhabi Economic Vision 2030 and maintains a strategic international presence across five continents. FAB is currently leading the region's sustainable finance efforts and is heavily investing in digital transformation to enhance its global corporate and transaction banking capabilities. Its headquarters are in Abu Dhabi, UAE.
