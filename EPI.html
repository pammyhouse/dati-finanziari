Dati finanziari per: WisdomTree India Earnings Fund (EPI)

EPI is the WisdomTree India Earnings Fund, an exchange-traded fund designed to provide exposure to profitable companies within the Indian equity market. Unlike traditional market-cap-weighted indices, EPI tracks the WisdomTree India Earnings Index, which selects and weights its components based on net income generated by companies in the previous fiscal year. This approach emphasizes corporate profitability and fundamental value within the rapidly expanding Indian economy, covering key sectors such as information technology, financials, and energy. The fund serves as a strategic instrument for investors seeking to capture the growth potential of India's developing market while focusing on companies with proven earnings strength. Its performance is driven by India’s GDP growth trends, infrastructure development, monetary policy decisions by the Reserve Bank of India, and global capital flows into emerging markets.
