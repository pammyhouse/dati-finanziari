Dati finanziari per: Shiba Inu (SHIB)

Shiba Inu (SHIB) is a decentralized, community-led cryptocurrency that evolved from a popular meme into a comprehensive ecosystem. Launched in 2020, it operates as an ERC-20 token on the Ethereum blockchain, granting it compatibility with a vast array of decentralized applications (dApps). In 2026, Shiba Inu has transitioned far beyond its "meme coin" origins, powered by its Layer 2 scaling solution, Shibarium, and a robust suite of platforms including ShibaSwap and the SHIB Metaverse. The ecosystem employs a multi-token model—comprising SHIB, LEASH, and BONE—to facilitate everything from micro-transactions to complex decentralized governance. Known for its massive and loyal "SHIB Army," the project continues to focus on community-driven development and charitable initiatives worldwide.
