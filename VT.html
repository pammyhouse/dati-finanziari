Dati finanziari per: Vanguard Total World Stock ETF (VT)

VT is the Vanguard Total World Stock ETF, a comprehensive fund designed to provide exposure to the entire global equity market. By tracking the FTSE Global All Cap Index, the ETF invests in thousands of companies across both developed and emerging markets, encompassing large-, mid-, and small-cap stocks. This structure offers unparalleled geographic and sector diversification, making it an ideal core holding for investors seeking to capture the aggregate performance of the world's economy. The fund's performance is driven by global macroeconomic trends, international trade relations, currency fluctuations, and the collective growth of corporate earnings across major financial markets. VT serves as a primary, low-cost instrument for long-term investors aiming to minimize regional risk and achieve broad-based global equity exposure.
