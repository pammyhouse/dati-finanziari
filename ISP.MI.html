Dati finanziari per: Intesa Sanpaolo S.p.A. (ISP.MI)

Intesa Sanpaolo S.p.A. is one of Italy’s largest banking groups, providing a full range of retail, corporate, and investment banking services. Founded in 2007 from the merger of Banca Intesa and Sanpaolo IMI, the bank is headquartered in Turin and Milan. It operates both nationally and internationally, offering banking, insurance, asset management, and wealth management services. It is publicly traded on the Borsa Italiana under the ticker ISP.MI and plays a central role in Italy’s financial system.
