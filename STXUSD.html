Dati finanziari per: Stacks (STXUSD)

Stacks is a Bitcoin Layer 2 that enables smart contracts and decentralized applications to use Bitcoin as an asset and settle transactions on the Bitcoin blockchain. By utilizing a unique consensus mechanism known as Proof of Transfer (PoX), Stacks bridges the gap between Bitcoin's unparalleled security and the flexibility of programmable contracts. In 2026, the network's Nakamoto release has significantly improved transaction speeds and finality, solidifying its role as a premier scaling solution for the Bitcoin ecosystem. The native STX token is used to pay for transaction fees and can be "stacked" by holders to earn Bitcoin rewards directly, incentivizing long-term participation and securing the network's connection to the primary Bitcoin chain.
