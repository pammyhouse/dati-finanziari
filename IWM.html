Dati finanziari per: iShares Russell 2000 ETF (IWM)

IWM represents the iShares Russell 2000 ETF, an exchange-traded fund managed by BlackRock that seeks to track the performance of the Russell 2000 Index, which measures the performance of approximately 2,000 U.S. small-cap companies. The fund provides broad exposure to the small-cap segment of the U.S. equity market, which is generally considered more sensitive to domestic economic conditions and business cycles. IWM is widely used by investors for diversification, tactical allocation, and expressing views on U.S. economic growth and risk appetite. Its performance is influenced by interest rates, credit conditions, inflation expectations, and overall investor sentiment toward small-cap equities.
