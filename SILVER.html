Dati finanziari per: Silver (SILVER)

Silver (XAG) is a precious metal traded globally, with its price quoted against the United States Dollar (USD) in the XAGUSD currency pair. Silver is used both as an investment asset and in industrial applications, including electronics, solar panels, and jewelry. The XAGUSD exchange rate is influenced by factors such as global economic conditions, inflation expectations, U.S. dollar strength, and supply-demand dynamics in the silver market. It is considered a safe-haven asset and a hedge against inflation.
