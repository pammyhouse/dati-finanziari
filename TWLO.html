Dati finanziari per: Twilio Inc. (TWLO)

Twilio Inc. is an American cloud communications platform‑as‑a‑service (CPaaS) company based in San Francisco, California, that provides programmable communication tools such as voice, messaging, video, and email via API to support consumer and business applications. It enables developers and enterprises to build interactive experiences with its global API infrastructure used by over 10 million developers and hundreds of thousands of companies worldwide. Founded in 2008, it offers services like Twilio SendGrid, Twilio Flex, and Twilio Segment. Twilio is headquartered in San Francisco, California.
