Dati finanziari per: HDFC Bank Limited (HDB)

HDFC Bank Limited is an Indian banking and financial services company headquartered in Mumbai, standing as India's largest private sector bank by assets and market capitalization. Incorporated in 1994, HDFC Bank was among the first to receive an 'in principle' approval from the Reserve Bank of India to set up a bank in the private sector. The bank offers a comprehensive suite of financial products and services catering to wholesale and retail customers, including commercial and investment banking on the wholesale side, and transactional/branch banking on the retail side. It possesses a massive nationwide network of branches and ATMs, extending its reach deep into semi-urban and rural areas. Following its historic 2023 merger with its parent company, Housing Development Finance Corporation (HDFC Ltd), the bank significantly expanded its mortgage lending portfolio, creating a financial behemoth uniquely positioned to capitalize on India's rapidly growing consumer credit and housing markets.
