Dati finanziari per: Telefónica, S.A. (TEF.MC)

Telefónica, S.A. is a Spanish multinational telecommunications company and one of the largest telephone operators and mobile network providers in the world. It operates under three main brands: Movistar (Spain and Latin America), O2 (United Kingdom and Germany), and Vivo (Brazil). The company is currently executing its "GPS" strategic plan (Growth, Profitability, and Sustainability), focusing on fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) expansion, 5G deployment, and the growth of its digital tech unit, Telefónica Tech. As a key player in the digital economy, Telefónica is also prioritizing the reduction of its debt through strategic asset spin-offs and infrastructure sharing. Its headquarters are in Madrid, Spain.
