Dati finanziari per: AstraZeneca PLC (AZN.L)

AstraZeneca PLC is a British-Swedish multinational pharmaceutical and biotechnology company that is a global leader in biopharmaceutical innovation. The company's primary focus lies in three core therapy areas: Oncology, Rare Diseases (strengthened by the acquisition of Alexion), and BioPharmaceuticals (comprising Cardiovascular, Renal & Metabolism, and Respiratory & Immunology). AstraZeneca is renowned for its robust R&D pipeline and its commitment to precision medicine and data-driven drug discovery. A major player in global health, the company maintains extensive manufacturing facilities and research centers worldwide, focusing on life-changing medicines that address complex unmet medical needs. Its corporate headquarters are located in Cambridge, United Kingdom.
