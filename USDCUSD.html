Dati finanziari per: USD Coin (USDCUSD)

USD Coin (USDC) is a leading digital stablecoin that is pegged to the United States Dollar on a 1:1 basis. Managed by Circle, USDC is fully backed by cash and short-dated U.S. government obligations, ensuring that it remains redeemable for fiat currency at all times. In 2026, USDC has solidified its position as the "regulatory gold standard" for stablecoins, following the full implementation of the GENIUS Act in the U.S. and MiCA in Europe. It serves as a critical bridge between traditional finance and the decentralized economy, widely used by institutional heavyweights like BlackRock and Visa for real-time treasury settlements. Operating across dozens of blockchains, USDC provides a stable, transparent, and highly liquid medium for global payments, DeFi lending, and as a secure store of value in the volatile crypto market.
