Dati finanziari per: Unipol Gruppo S.p.A. (UNI.MI)

Unipol Gruppo S.p.A. is a leading Italian financial services holding company, primarily operating in the insurance and banking sectors. Headquartered in Bologna, Unipol is the undisputed leader in the Italian Non-Life insurance market, particularly dominating the Motor Vehicle (MV) insurance segment through its core subsidiary, UnipolSai Assicurazioni. The group also holds a significant presence in the Life insurance sector, offering pension, savings, and welfare products. Unipol distinguishes itself through a highly diversified business model that extends beyond traditional insurance. The group operates a robust "Mobility Ecosystem" encompassing electronic tolling (UnipolMove), vehicle rental, and maintenance services. Furthermore, it has established "Welfare" and "Property" ecosystems, which include a network of medical clinics, real estate management, and the Gruppo UNA hotel chain. Unipol also maintains strategic bancassurance partnerships and significant equity stakes in major Italian banks, cementing its role as a pivotal player in Italy's broader financial landscape.
