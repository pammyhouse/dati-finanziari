Dati finanziari per: Royal Bank of Canada (RY)

Royal Bank of Canada (RBC) is a Canadian multinational financial services company and the largest bank in Canada by market capitalization and assets. Founded in 1864 and headquartered in Toronto, Ontario (with a historic head office in Montreal), RBC is a globally systemically important bank (G-SIB). It operates a highly diversified business model encompassing Personal & Commercial Banking, Wealth Management, Insurance, Investor & Treasury Services, and Capital Markets. In Canada, RBC dominates the retail banking landscape with an extensive network of branches and ATMs. Internationally, it boasts a formidable Wealth Management division, particularly in the United States (following the acquisition of City National Bank) and the UK, alongside a strong global Capital Markets arm. RBC recently fortified its domestic dominance through the landmark acquisition of HSBC Bank Canada, further expanding its client base and commercial banking capabilities. The bank is recognized for its strong capital ratios, consistent dividend history, and conservative risk management.
