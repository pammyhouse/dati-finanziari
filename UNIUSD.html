Dati finanziari per: Uniswap (UNIUSD)

Uniswap is the world's leading decentralized exchange (DEX) and a cornerstone of the Decentralized Finance (DeFi) ecosystem. Operating on the Ethereum blockchain and multiple Layer 2 solutions, Uniswap utilizes an automated market maker (AMM) model to enable permissionless token swaps without the need for traditional order books or centralized intermediaries. In 2026, Uniswap remains a dominant force with its V4 protocol and the expansion of its own Unichain network, offering enhanced customizability through "hooks" and optimized gas efficiency. The native governance token, UNI, allows holders to participate in the decision-making processes of the Uniswap DAO, including treasury management, protocol upgrades, and fee structure adjustments, reinforcing its status as a vital public good for the open financial system.
