Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI Netherlands ETF (EWN)

EWN is the iShares MSCI Netherlands ETF, an investment vehicle that tracks the MSCI Netherlands Investable Market Index. The fund offers targeted exposure to large-, mid-, and small-cap companies within the Dutch equity market. By investing in EWN, participants gain access to key sectors including technology, consumer staples, and financials, heavily weighted towards multinational corporations listed on the Euronext Amsterdam. The ETF's performance is driven by the overall health of the Dutch economy, developments in global trade and logistics, European regulatory changes, and the competitive positioning of major Dutch technology and financial firms. It is a preferred instrument for investors seeking to diversify their European holdings with exposure to one of the most open and export-oriented economies in the region.
