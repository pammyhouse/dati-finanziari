Dati finanziari per: Emaar Properties PJSC (EMAAR.DU)

Emaar Properties PJSC is a major Emirati multinational real estate development company and a symbol of Dubai's rapid urban transformation. The company is best known for developing the Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building, and the Dubai Mall, one of the world's largest shopping centers. Emaar operates through three primary segments: Real Estate (residential and commercial development), Leasing (shopping malls and retail), and Hospitality & Leisure. The company has a significant international footprint, with large-scale projects in Egypt, India, Saudi Arabia, and Turkey. Emaar's strategy is currently focused on high-margin luxury residential launches and expanding its recurring income from its malls and hospitality assets. Its headquarters are in Dubai, UAE.
