Dati finanziari per: Novo Nordisk A/S (NVO)

Novo Nordisk A/S is a Danish multinational pharmaceutical company headquartered in Bagsværd, Denmark, and stands as a global leader in diabetes and obesity care. Founded in 1923, the company has nearly a century of expertise in discovering, developing, and manufacturing biological medicines. Novo Nordisk currently controls a massive share of the global insulin market. However, in recent years, the company has experienced explosive growth and achieved the status of Europe's most valuable company due to its groundbreaking GLP-1 receptor agonist therapies. Drugs like Ozempic (for type 2 diabetes) and Wegovy (for weight management) have shown unprecedented efficacy, creating a massive new therapeutic market for obesity. Beyond diabetes and weight management, Novo Nordisk also develops treatments for rare blood disorders (such as hemophilia) and rare endocrine diseases. The company is characterized by its unique ownership structure, being controlled by the Novo Nordisk Foundation, which ensures long-term strategic focus and extensive philanthropic contributions to scientific research.
