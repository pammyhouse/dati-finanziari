Dati finanziari per: Orca (ORCA)

Orca is one of the most user-friendly decentralized exchanges (DEX) built on the Solana blockchain. It allows users to swap tokens, provide liquidity, and earn yield through its concentrated liquidity pools, known as Whirlpools. Orca is recognized for its emphasis on human-centric design, offering features like the Fair Price Indicator to ensure users get the best possible rates. As a core component of the Solana DeFi ecosystem, Orca leverages the network's high speed and low transaction costs to provide an efficient trading experience. The native ORCA token is primarily used for governance, allowing holders to participate in decisions regarding protocol fees, treasury management, and future development of the platform.
