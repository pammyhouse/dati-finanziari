Dati finanziari per: NDX (NDX)

The Nasdaq 100 Index (NDX) includes 100 of the largest domestic and international non-financial companies listed on the Nasdaq Stock Market, representing a wide range of industries including technology, consumer services, healthcare, and telecommunications. It is a modified market-capitalization weighted index, designed to reflect the performance of innovative growth companies that drive the modern economy. The index serves as a benchmark for many investment funds and ETFs, providing investors exposure to some of the most influential and fast-growing firms in the technology and consumer sectors. Key constituents include Apple, Microsoft, Amazon, Tesla, and Alphabet, among others. The Nasdaq 100 is widely followed by investors globally and plays a critical role in market sentiment and economic forecasting.
