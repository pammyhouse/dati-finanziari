Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI United Kingdom ETF (EWU)

EWU is the iShares MSCI United Kingdom ETF, designed to provide broad exposure to large- and mid-cap companies within the U.K. equity market. The fund tracks the MSCI United Kingdom Index and includes major global corporations across sectors such as financials, consumer staples, energy, and healthcare. By investing in EWU, market participants gain access to equities listed on the London Stock Exchange, offering a diversified approach to the British economy. The ETF's performance is closely linked to U.K. domestic economic data, Bank of England interest rate decisions, global commodity price trends—particularly in the energy sector—and the evolving trade relationship between the U.K. and international markets. It serves as a core tool for investors seeking to integrate British equity exposure into their global asset allocation.
