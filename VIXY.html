Dati finanziari per: ProShares VIX Short-Term Futures ETF (VIXY)

VIXY is the ProShares VIX Short-Term Futures ETF, an investment vehicle designed to provide exposure to the CBOE Volatility Index (VIX), often referred to as the "fear gauge." Unlike traditional equity ETFs, VIXY does not hold stocks; instead, it invests in short-term futures contracts on the VIX. This ETF is used by traders and institutional investors primarily as a tactical tool to hedge against market downturns or to speculate on spikes in market volatility. Because of the inherent structure of futures markets—specifically the phenomenon of contango—VIXY is generally intended for short-term trading rather than long-term buy-and-hold strategies. Its performance is highly sensitive to rapid shifts in investor sentiment, sudden equity market corrections, and geopolitical events that drive uncertainty in the S&P 500 index.
