Dati finanziari per: Itaú Unibanco Holding S.A. (ITUB)

Itaú Unibanco Holding S.A. is a Brazilian financial services company and the largest banking institution in Latin America and the Southern Hemisphere. Resulting from the merger of Banco Itaú and Unibanco in 2008, the bank provides a wide range of retail, corporate, and investment banking services across 18 countries. Itaú is a leader in digital banking innovation in Brazil, maintaining a strong focus on customer-centric technology and wealth management. The bank is currently navigating a competitive landscape of fintech disruption by accelerating its own digital transformation and expanding its ESG-led credit portfolios. Its headquarters are in São Paulo, Brazil.
