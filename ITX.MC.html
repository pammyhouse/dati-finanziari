Dati finanziari per: Industria de Diseño Textil, S.A. (ITX.MC)

Industria de Diseño Textil, S.A., known as Inditex, is a Spanish multinational clothing company and the world's largest fashion retailer. It operates over 7,200 stores in 93 markets worldwide through its flagship brand Zara, as well as Pull&Bear, Massimo Dutti, Bershka, Stradivarius, Oysho, and Zara Home. Inditex is renowned for its "fast fashion" business model, which relies on a highly flexible supply chain and localized production to bring new designs from the drawing board to the shop floor in just a few weeks. The company is currently investing heavily in its integrated "Store & Online" platform and sustainable textile innovation. Its headquarters are in Arteixo, Spain.
