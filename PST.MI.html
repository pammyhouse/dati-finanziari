Dati finanziari per: Poste Italiane S.p.A. (PST.MI)

Poste Italiane S.p.A. is Italy’s leading provider of postal and financial services. Founded in 1862 and headquartered in Rome, the company operates through a wide range of segments including mail, logistics, insurance, payments, mobile, and banking services. With a vast network of branches and digital platforms, Poste Italiane plays a crucial role in Italy’s infrastructure and economy. It is publicly traded on the Borsa Italiana under the ticker PST.MI and is considered a strategic asset for the country, also serving as a key distributor of government services and pensions.
