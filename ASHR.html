Dati finanziari per: Xtrackers Harvest CSI 300 China A-Shares ETF (ASHR)

ASHR is the Xtrackers Harvest CSI 300 China A-Shares ETF, which seeks to track the CSI 300 Index, composed of the 300 largest and most liquid stocks listed on the Shanghai and Shenzhen Stock Exchanges in mainland China. Unlike funds that hold Chinese stocks listed in Hong Kong or the U.S., ASHR provides direct exposure to "A-shares," offering a truer reflection of China's domestic equity market across vital sectors such as financials, consumer staples, and information technology. The fund is a critical instrument for investors aiming to capture the growth of the world's second-largest economy from within. Its performance is heavily influenced by domestic economic policy shifts, regulatory changes from the China Securities Regulatory Commission (CSRC), infrastructure investment levels, and the broader geopolitical landscape affecting mainland markets.
