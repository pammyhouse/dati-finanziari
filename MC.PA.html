Dati finanziari per: LVMH Moët Hennessy Louis Vuitton SE (MC.PA)

LVMH Moët Hennessy Louis Vuitton SE is a French multinational luxury goods conglomerate and the world's most valuable luxury group by market capitalization. The company manages a unique portfolio of over 75 prestigious "Maisons" divided into six sectors: Fashion and Leather Goods (including Louis Vuitton and Dior), Watches and Jewelry (Tiffany & Co., Bulgari), Wines and Spirits (Moët & Chandon, Hennessy), Perfumes and Cosmetics, Selective Retailing (Sephora), and Other Activities. LVMH is a benchmark for the global luxury market, driven by its strategy of creative autonomy and long-term brand equity. The group is increasingly focused on high-end experiential luxury and sustainable sourcing of raw materials. Its headquarters are in Paris, France.
