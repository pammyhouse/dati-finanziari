Dati finanziari per: Moncler S.p.A. (MONC.MI)

Moncler S.p.A. is an Italian high-end luxury fashion house known globally for its iconic down jackets and premium sportswear. Founded in 1952 in the Alpine village of Monestier-de-Clermont, France (from which the name "Moncler" is derived), the brand originally produced sleeping bags and specialized mountain gear. The company was acquired in 2003 by Italian entrepreneur Remo Ruffini, who orchestrated a spectacular turnaround, repositioning Moncler from a niche outdoor brand into a global luxury fashion powerhouse. Headquartered in Milan, Italy, Moncler blends extreme-weather technical performance with haute couture aesthetics. The brand's strategy is heavily driven by its "Moncler Genius" project, which involves rolling collaborations with world-renowned designers to create unique, hype-driven collections. In 2020, Moncler expanded its portfolio by acquiring the cult Italian luxury streetwear brand Stone Island. The company operates a highly profitable direct-to-consumer model, leveraging a network of exclusive boutiques and a strong digital presence across the Americas, Europe, and Asia.
