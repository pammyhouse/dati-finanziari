Dati finanziari per: Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SPCX)

Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SpaceX) designs, develops, and launches advanced aerospace systems and space transportation technologies. The company focuses on reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars through fully reusable rocket systems. SpaceX develops and operates the Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy launch vehicles, the Dragon spacecraft for cargo and crew missions, and the Starship spacecraft currently under development for deep-space exploration. The company also operates Starlink, a large satellite constellation designed to provide global high-speed broadband internet access, including in remote and underserved regions. SpaceX provides launch services for commercial satellite operators, government agencies, and international organizations, and continues to innovate in propulsion systems, orbital deployment technologies, and reusable spacecraft engineering. Founded in 2002 by Elon Musk, SpaceX is headquartered in Hawthorne, California.
