Dati finanziari per: Broadcom Inc. (AVGO)

Broadcom Inc. is a premier global technology leader that designs, develops, and supplies a broad portfolio of semiconductor and infrastructure software solutions. The company's origins trace back to the technical heritage of AT&T/Bell Labs, Lucent, and Hewlett-Packard/Agilent, though its modern iteration was formed following the acquisition of Broadcom Corporation by Avago Technologies in 2016. Today, Broadcom serves critical markets including data center, networking, enterprise software, broadband, wireless, storage, and industrial applications. Its semiconductor segment produces complex system-on-a-chip (SoC) components, RF filters, and custom ASICs, heavily utilized in smartphones, routers, and data center switches. In recent years, Broadcom has aggressively expanded its infrastructure software business through high-profile acquisitions, including CA Technologies, Symantec's enterprise security business, and most notably, VMware. This strategic pivot allows Broadcom to offer end-to-end solutions, from silicon hardware to hybrid cloud and cybersecurity software.
