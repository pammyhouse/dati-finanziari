Dati finanziari per: Banco Santander, S.A. (SAN.MC)

Banco Santander, S.A. is a Spanish multinational financial services company and one of the largest banking institutions in the Eurozone. The group maintains a significant presence in ten core markets across Europe and the Americas, operating a diversified model that balances retail banking, consumer finance, and investment banking. Santander is a leader in digital banking transformation in Latin America and is recognized for its massive scale in Brazil and Mexico. The bank is currently focusing on simplifying its global product offering and scaling its "One Santander" initiative to enhance cross-border operational efficiency and profitability. Its headquarters are in Santander and Madrid, Spain.
