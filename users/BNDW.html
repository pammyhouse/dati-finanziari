Dati finanziari per: Vanguard Total World Bond ETF (BNDW)

BNDW is the Vanguard Total World Bond ETF, a fund designed to provide broad, diversified exposure to the global investment-grade bond market. By tracking the Bloomberg Global Aggregate Float Adjusted Composite Index, the ETF invests in a combination of U.S. and non-U.S. dollar-denominated investment-grade bonds, including government, agency, corporate, and securitized instruments. This fund serves as a comprehensive tool for investors seeking to capture the aggregate performance of the world's fixed-income markets while mitigating idiosyncratic regional risks through wide geographic and issuer diversification. Its performance is primarily driven by global interest rate fluctuations, central bank monetary policy shifts, inflation expectations, and changes in credit risk spreads across both developed and emerging markets, offering a foundational asset for a globally diversified portfolio.
