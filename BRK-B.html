Dati finanziari per: Berkshire Hathaway Inc. (BRK-B)

Berkshire Hathaway Inc. is a globally recognized multinational conglomerate holding company headquartered in Omaha, Nebraska, under the leadership of Chairman and CEO Warren Buffett. Originally a textile manufacturing company, Berkshire has evolved into one of the largest public companies in the world. The firm owns a vast array of businesses operating across highly diverse sectors. Its core operations include insurance and reinsurance (GEICO, General Re, Berkshire Hathaway Primary Group), freight rail transportation (BNSF Railway), and utility and energy generation and distribution (Berkshire Hathaway Energy). Additionally, the company boasts extensive manufacturing, service, and retail operations, including well-known brands like Dairy Queen, Duracell, Fruit of the Loom, and Precision Castparts. Beyond its wholly-owned subsidiaries, Berkshire is famous for its massive equity investment portfolio, holding significant stakes in industry giants such as Apple, Bank of America, American Express, and The Coca-Cola Company.
