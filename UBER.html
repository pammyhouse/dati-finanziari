Dati finanziari per: Uber Technologies, Inc. (UBER)

Uber Technologies, Inc. develops and operates proprietary technology applications in the United States, Canada, Latin America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. It operates through three segments: Mobility, Delivery, and Freight. The Mobility segment connects consumers with drivers who provide rides in a range of vehicles, while the Delivery segment allows consumers to search for and order food from restaurants, groceries, and other local merchants. The Freight segment connects carriers with shippers for freight transportation. Uber also offers financial services and other technology solutions. The company was founded in 2009 and is headquartered in San Francisco, California.
