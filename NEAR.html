Dati finanziari per: Near Protocol (NEAR)

Near Protocol is a decentralized application platform designed to make apps usable on the web. The network runs on a Proof-of-Stake (PoS) consensus mechanism called Nightshade, which is designed to offer dynamic scalability and cross-shard interoperability. Its focus on developer and user-friendliness is central to its goal of accelerating the world's transition to open technologies. Near features human-readable account names, as opposed to the cryptographic wallet addresses common to most blockchains. The native token, NEAR, is used to pay for transaction fees and storage on the platform, and can also be staked by token holders to participate in network governance.
