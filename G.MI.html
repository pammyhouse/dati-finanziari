Dati finanziari per: Assicurazioni Generali S.p.A. (G.MI)

Assicurazioni Generali S.p.A. is a prominent Italian insurance and asset management company, and ranks as one of the largest global insurance providers. Founded in Trieste in 1831, Generali has built a commanding presence across Europe, with particularly strong market shares in Italy, Germany, and France, while also expanding its footprint in Asia and Latin America. The group operates through two primary segments: Life Insurance, offering savings, family protection, and unit-linked policies, and Property & Casualty (Non-Life) Insurance, providing coverage for auto, home, accident, and commercial risks. Beyond traditional insurance, Generali has significantly grown its Asset Management division, catering to both institutional and retail clients to diversify its revenue streams. Known for its financial solidity and extensive agency network, Generali plays a critical systemic role in the European financial sector and continuously invests in digital transformation and sustainable, ESG-compliant investment strategies.
