Dati finanziari per: HSBC Holdings plc (HSBA.L)

HSBC Holdings plc is a British multinational universal bank and financial services holding company. It is the largest bank in Europe and one of the largest in the world, maintaining a unique strategic bridge between Western and Eastern markets, with a dominant presence in Asia (particularly Hong Kong). The bank operates through three main business groups: Wealth and Personal Banking, Commercial Banking, and Global Banking and Markets. HSBC is currently focusing on its "Pivot to Asia" strategy, reallocating capital to high-growth markets and expanding its digital wealth management services for the emerging middle class. The institution is a key facilitator of international trade and sustainable finance global initiatives. Its headquarters are in London, United Kingdom.
