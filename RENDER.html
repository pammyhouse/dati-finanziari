Dati finanziari per: Render (RENDER)

The Render Network is the leading provider of decentralized GPU-based rendering solutions, designed to connect users in need of GPU compute power with providers who have idle resources. Originally built on Ethereum and later migrated to Solana for increased scalability, the protocol powers next-generation digital content creation, including 3D rendering, AI model training, and spatial computing. In 2026, Render remains a critical infrastructure for the Metaverse and generative AI industries, utilizing its native RENDER token to facilitate payments, reward node operators, and enable community governance. By parallelizing complex rendering tasks across a global network, Render offers a cost-effective and high-performance alternative to centralized cloud computing providers.
