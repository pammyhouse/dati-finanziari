Dati finanziari per: S&P 500 (SPX500)

The S&P 500 Index (SPX500) is a leading benchmark for the U.S. equity market, comprising 500 of the largest publicly traded companies across various industries including technology, healthcare, financials, consumer discretionary, and industrials. It is a market capitalization-weighted index that provides a comprehensive overview of the overall market performance and is widely used by investors to gauge economic health and portfolio performance. The index includes blue-chip companies such as Apple, Microsoft, Amazon, and Johnson & Johnson, representing a diverse and balanced cross-section of the U.S. economy. It serves as the foundation for numerous investment products including ETFs, mutual funds, and derivatives, making it one of the most closely followed indices globally.
