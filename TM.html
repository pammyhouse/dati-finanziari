Dati finanziari per: Toyota Motor Corporation (TM)

Toyota Motor Corporation is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer headquartered in Toyota City, Aichi, Japan, and stands as one of the largest and most influential automobile manufacturers globally. Founded in 1937 by Kiichiro Toyoda, the company is renowned for the Toyota Production System, which revolutionized modern manufacturing through principles of lean production and "just-in-time" inventory. Toyota designs, manufactures, and sells a diverse range of vehicles across its flagship Toyota brand, the luxury Lexus marque, and subsidiaries Daihatsu and Hino Motors. The company has historically been the pioneer of hybrid-electric technology, famously introducing the Prius in 1997. While it maintains a dominant position in hybrid and plug-in hybrid markets, Toyota is currently executing a multi-pathway strategy for carbon neutrality, heavily investing in battery electric vehicles (BEVs), hydrogen fuel cell technology, and solid-state battery research, alongside broad mobility services and autonomous driving technologies.
