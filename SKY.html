Dati finanziari per: Sky (SKY)

Sky (SKY) is the native governance and utility token of the Sky Protocol, representing the next generation of the platform formerly known as MakerDAO. Launched to provide a more accessible and community-driven DeFi experience, Sky centers on its decentralized stablecoin, USDS, and offers users enhanced opportunities for savings and rewards. The protocol maintains its heritage as a pillar of the Ethereum ecosystem while introducing a refined governance model where SKY holders vote on key parameters such as risk management and interest rates. By integrating sophisticated smart contracts and a dynamic staking engine, Sky aims to bridge the gap between complex decentralized finance and user-friendly financial tools, fostering a versatile and scalable ecosystem for global participants.
