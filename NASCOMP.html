Dati finanziari per: Nasdaq Composite (NASCOMP)

The Nasdaq Composite Index (NASCOMP) is a broad-based, market capitalization-weighted index that includes more than 3,000 common equities listed on the Nasdaq stock exchange, encompassing a wide range of sectors with a strong emphasis on technology and innovation-driven companies such as Apple, Microsoft, Amazon, and Tesla. It serves as a key indicator of the performance of growth-oriented stocks and the tech industry, reflecting investor sentiment and trends within rapidly evolving markets. Known for its higher volatility relative to other indices, the Nasdaq Composite is widely followed by traders, investors, and financial analysts globally.
