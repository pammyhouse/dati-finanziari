Snowflake Inc. offers a cloud-based data platform that enables data warehousing, data engineering, data science, data application development, and data sharing. Its platform separates compute and storage, allowing scalable and concurrent analytics workloads. Snowflake serves customers across sectors such as technology, retail, healthcare, and financial services. The company went public in 2020 and is headquartered in Bozeman, Montana.
