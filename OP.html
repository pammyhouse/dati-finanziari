Dati finanziari per: Optimism (OP)

Optimism (OP) is a leading Layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum, designed to empower developers and users with fast, low-cost transactions while maintaining the security of the Ethereum mainnet. It operates as an Optimistic Rollup, processing transactions off-chain and periodically submitting compressed data to the Layer 1. In 2026, Optimism remains a central pillar of the "Superchain" vision, an integrated network of chains sharing the OP Stack to foster interoperability and shared security. The native OP token is the primary vehicle for governance within the Optimism Collective, enabling holders to participate in decisions regarding protocol upgrades, treasury management, and the distribution of retroactive public goods funding.
