Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI Japan ETF (EWJ)

EWJ is the iShares MSCI Japan ETF, an investment fund that tracks an index of large- and mid-cap Japanese equities. It provides exposure to a wide spectrum of the Japanese economy, including prominent sectors such as industrials, consumer discretionary, and technology, featuring many of the world's leading automotive and electronics manufacturers. By investing in EWJ, market participants gain access to companies listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange. The performance of the ETF is heavily influenced by the monetary policies of the Bank of Japan, fluctuations in the Japanese Yen, domestic demographic trends, and global demand for Japanese manufactured goods. It serves as a primary tool for investors looking to incorporate one of Asia's most sophisticated and developed equity markets into their international asset allocation strategy.
