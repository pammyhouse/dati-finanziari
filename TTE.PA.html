Dati finanziari per: TotalEnergies SE (TTE.PA)

TotalEnergies SE is a French multinational integrated energy and petroleum company and one of the world's seven "supermajor" oil companies. Its activities cover the entire oil and gas chain, from exploration and production to refining, marketing, and trading. TotalEnergies is notably distinguished by its aggressive transition toward a multi-energy strategy, significantly increasing its investments in electricity, renewable energy (wind and solar), and low-carbon fuels. The company is a global leader in Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) and aims to reach net-zero emissions by 2050. TotalEnergies maintains a strong presence in Africa and the Middle East while expanding its retail and charging networks in Europe. Its headquarters are in Courbevoie, France.
