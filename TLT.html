Dati finanziari per: iShares 20+ Year Treasury Bond ETF (TLT)

TLT represents the iShares 20+ Year Treasury Bond ETF, managed by BlackRock, which tracks long-term U.S. Treasury bonds with maturities greater than 20 years. The fund provides exposure to U.S. government debt and is highly sensitive to changes in interest rates, inflation expectations, and Federal Reserve monetary policy. TLT is commonly used for portfolio diversification, income generation, and as a hedge against equity market volatility.
