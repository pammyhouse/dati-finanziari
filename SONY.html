Dati finanziari per: Sony Group Corporation (SONY)

Sony Group Corporation is a Japanese multinational conglomerate and one of the world's most comprehensive entertainment and technology companies, headquartered in Minato, Tokyo. Originally founded in 1946 as a humble electronics shop, Sony has grown into a global powerhouse operating across diverse and synergistic segments. Its Game & Network Services division, driven by the PlayStation brand, is a dominant force in the global video game industry. In entertainment, Sony Pictures produces and distributes major theatrical motion pictures and television programming, while Sony Music represents one of the largest recording labels and music publishing businesses worldwide. On the hardware side, the Entertainment Technology & Services segment produces high-end televisions, audio equipment, and digital cameras. Crucially, Sony's Imaging & Sensing Solutions division is a market leader in CMOS image sensors, supplying essential components for smartphones globally, including Apple's iPhones. The company also operates a significant Financial Services division in Japan.
