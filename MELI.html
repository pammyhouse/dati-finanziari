Dati finanziari per: MercadoLibre, Inc. (MELI)

MercadoLibre, Inc. is an Argentine multinational company incorporated in the United States that operates the largest online commerce and payments ecosystem in Latin America. Often described as the "Amazon of Latin America," the company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Montevideo, Argentina, with massive operations in Brazil, Mexico, Colombia, and Chile. The company's core marketplace platform, Mercado Libre, connects millions of buyers and sellers for a vast array of goods. To support this, the company developed Mercado Envios, a highly efficient, proprietary logistics and shipping network that has revolutionized delivery times across the continent. Crucially, the company also operates Mercado Pago, initially created to process marketplace payments but now evolved into a comprehensive digital wallet and fintech platform offering credit, savings, and point-of-sale solutions for millions of unbanked and underbanked individuals and small businesses throughout Latin America, driving massive parallel growth.
