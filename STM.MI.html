Dati finanziari per: STMicroelectronics N.V. (STM.MI)

STMicroelectronics N.V. is a multinational electronics and semiconductor manufacturer, ranking as one of the largest semiconductor companies in Europe. Headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, and with deep historical roots in Italy and France, STMicroelectronics designs, develops, manufactures, and markets a broad range of semiconductor integrated circuits and discrete devices. The company is a crucial supplier to the automotive and industrial sectors. It is a global leader in Silicon Carbide (SiC) technology, which is vital for producing the high-efficiency power modules used in electric vehicles (EVs) and renewable energy systems. Additionally, ST is renowned for its STM32 family of 32-bit microcontrollers, which act as the "brains" for countless embedded systems, IoT devices, and consumer electronics. The company's portfolio also encompasses sensors, MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems), and analog chips. By maintaining its own fabrication facilities (an "IDM" or Integrated Device Manufacturer model), ST ensures tight control over its supply chain and advanced manufacturing processes.
