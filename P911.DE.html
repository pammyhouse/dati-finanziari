Dr. Ing. h.c. F. Porsche AG, commonly referred to as Porsche, is a prestigious German automobile manufacturer specializing in high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and luxury sedans. Founded by Ferdinand Porsche in 1931 and headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany, the brand is synonymous with precision engineering, motorsport heritage, and iconic design, most notably embodied by its legendary 911 model. Today, Porsche's lineup has expanded successfully to include the Cayenne and Macan SUVs, the Panamera sports sedan, and the fully electric Taycan. The company operates as a crown jewel within the Volkswagen Group, which took full control of Porsche in 2012. In 2022, Porsche AG completed a highly successful initial public offering (IPO), though Volkswagen retains a majority stake, and Porsche Automobil Holding SE (controlled by the Porsche-Piëch families) holds a significant blocking minority. Porsche is currently executing an aggressive electrification strategy, aiming for a large majority of its sales to be fully electric by the end of the decade, while maintaining its industry-leading profit margins.
