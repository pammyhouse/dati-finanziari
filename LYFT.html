Dati finanziari per: Lyft, Inc. (LYFT)

Lyft, Inc. is an American company offering ride-hailing services, motorized scooters, and bicycle-sharing systems in the United States and Canada. It connects riders with drivers via its mobile app and employs dynamic pricing based on supply and demand. Lyft is the second‑largest ridesharing company in the U.S., and has expanded into bike and scooter rentals and partnerships with public transit agencies. It filed its 10‑K for 2024 and achieved profitability in 2024. Lyft was founded in 2012 and is headquartered in San Francisco, California.
