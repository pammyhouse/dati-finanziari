Dati finanziari per: Unilever PLC (ULVR.L)

Unilever PLC is a British multinational fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) company, holding a massive portfolio of over 400 brands in the nutrition, hygiene, and personal care categories. Some of its most iconic global brands include Dove, Knorr, Hellmann's, Magnum, and Ben & Jerry's. The company operates through five specialized business groups: Beauty & Wellbeing, Personal Care, Home Care, Nutrition, and Ice Cream. Unilever is highly focused on emerging markets, which account for a significant portion of its revenue, and is currently implementing a "Growth Action Plan" to sharpen its brand focus and improve operational margins through digital supply chain optimization and sustainability-led innovation. Its headquarters are in London, United Kingdom.
