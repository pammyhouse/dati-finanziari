Dati finanziari per: BNP Paribas S.A. (BNP.PA)

BNP Paribas S.A. is a French multinational universal bank and the largest banking group in the Eurozone by total assets. The bank operates through three main business areas: Corporate & Institutional Banking (CIB), Commercial, Personal Banking & Services (CPBS), and Investment & Protection Services (IPS). BNP Paribas is a key systemic institution in the global financial market, maintaining a dominant position in European investment banking and asset management. The group is a leader in sustainable finance and has been accelerating its transition away from fossil fuel financing toward green energy projects. Following its exit from the US retail market, the bank is focusing on strengthening its European leadership and digital banking platforms. Its headquarters are in Paris, France.
