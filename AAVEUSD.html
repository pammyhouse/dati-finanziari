Dati finanziari per: Aave (AAVEUSD)

Aave is a decentralized non-custodial liquidity protocol that allows users to participate as depositors or borrowers. Depositors provide liquidity to the market to earn a passive income, while borrowers are able to borrow in an overcollateralized (perpetual) or undercollateralized (flash loan) fashion. Originally launched as ETHLend in 2017, the protocol rebranded to Aave and has since become one of the foundational pillars of the DeFi ecosystem. It supports a wide range of assets and features innovative financial products such as stable interest rates and credit delegation. The native governance token, AAVE, allows holders to vote on protocol improvements and provides security to the network through the Safety Module, earning rewards in exchange for protecting the protocol against shortfall events.
