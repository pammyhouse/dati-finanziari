Dati finanziari per: BP p.l.c. (BP.L)

BP p.l.c. is a British multinational oil and gas "supermajor" and a significant player in the global energy market. The company operates through three main segments: Gas & Low Carbon Energy, Customers & Products, and Oil Production & Operations. BP is aggressively reorienting its strategy toward an integrated energy model, increasing investments in renewable energy, bioenergy, and electric vehicle charging networks through its BP Pulse brand. Despite its traditional fossil fuel roots, the company aims to significantly reduce its carbon footprint while optimizing its high-value upstream oil and gas assets. BP serves millions of customers daily through its retail network and convenience stores worldwide. Its headquarters are in London, United Kingdom.
