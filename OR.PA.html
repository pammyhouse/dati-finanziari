Dati finanziari per: L'Oréal S.A. (OR.PA)

L'Oréal S.A. is a French multinational cosmetics and personal care company and the world's largest beauty group. The company operates a vast portfolio divided into four divisions: Consumer Products (L'Oréal Paris, Maybelline), L'Oréal Luxe (Lancôme, Yves Saint Laurent), Professional Products, and Dermatological Beauty (La Roche-Posay, CeraVe). L'Oréal is a pioneer in "Beauty Tech," utilizing AI and data analytics to offer personalized skin-care and makeup solutions. The group is recognized for its massive R&D budget, consistently driving innovation in high-performance cosmetic formulas and sustainable packaging. With a presence in over 150 countries, the company is highly focused on the premiumization of the Asian market. Its headquarters are in Clichy, France.
