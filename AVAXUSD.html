Dati finanziari per: Avalanche (AVAXUSD)

Avalanche (AVAX) is a high-performance, scalable, and secure blockchain platform designed for decentralized applications (dApps) and custom blockchain networks. It is widely known for its unique consensus mechanism, which enables near-instant transaction finality and massive throughput, processing thousands of transactions per second. Avalanche is composed of three built-in blockchains: the X-Chain, P-Chain, and C-Chain, each serving a specific purpose in asset creation, platform governance, and smart contract execution. The native utility token, AVAX, is used for securing the network through staking, paying for transaction fees, and providing a basic unit of account between the multiple subnets created on the platform.
