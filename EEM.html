Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI Emerging Markets ETF (EEM)

EEM represents the iShares MSCI Emerging Markets ETF, managed by BlackRock, which tracks the MSCI Emerging Markets Index, providing exposure to large- and mid-cap companies across developing economies including China, India, Brazil, South Africa, and other emerging countries. The fund offers investors access to high-growth markets with higher volatility and risk compared to developed markets. Its performance is influenced by global economic growth, commodity prices, currency fluctuations, political stability, and international capital flows.
