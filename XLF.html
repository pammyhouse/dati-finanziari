Dati finanziari per: Financial Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLF)

XLF represents the Financial Select Sector SPDR Fund, which tracks the financial sector of the S&P 500 Index, including banks, insurance companies, and diversified financial firms. The fund is widely used to gain targeted exposure to the U.S. financial industry and is influenced by interest rates, credit cycles, regulatory changes, and overall economic activity.
