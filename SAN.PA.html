Dati finanziari per: Sanofi S.A. (SAN.PA)

Sanofi S.A. is a French multinational pharmaceutical company and one of the world's leading healthcare providers. The company focuses on four primary business units: Specialty Care (immunology, oncology, rare diseases), Vaccines, General Medicine, and Consumer Healthcare. Sanofi's flagship product, Dupixent, has become a global leader in treating inflammatory conditions. The company is currently undergoing a strategic transformation named "Play to Win," which involves reallocating capital toward innovative R&D, particularly in mRNA technology and immunology, while divesting from some of its non-core consumer health assets. Sanofi maintains a massive global manufacturing footprint and is a critical player in global public health initiatives. Its headquarters are in Paris, France.
