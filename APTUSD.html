Dati finanziari per: Aptos (APTUSD)

Aptos (APT) is a Layer 1 Proof-of-Stake blockchain designed for scalability, safety, and reliability. Developed by Aptos Labs—a team founded by former Meta engineers who worked on the Diem project—the network utilizes the Move programming language to ensure secure and flexible asset management. Aptos distinguishes itself through its parallel execution engine, Block-STM, which allows it to process multiple transactions simultaneously, significantly reducing latency and increasing throughput compared to traditional serial execution blockchains. The native APT token is used to pay for network transaction fees, participate in governance votes, and secure the network through staking, aiming to provide a high-performance infrastructure for decentralized applications and digital assets.
