ENI S.p.A. is an Italian multinational energy company headquartered in Rome. Founded in 1953, ENI operates in the oil, natural gas, and renewable energy sectors across more than 60 countries. The company is listed on the Borsa Italiana under the ticker ENI.MI and plays a major role in Europe’s energy supply, while increasingly investing in sustainable and low-carbon energy solutions.
