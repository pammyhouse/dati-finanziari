Dati finanziari per: Shanghai Composite Index (CHN50)

The Shanghai Composite Index (CHN50) is the main stock market index tracking all stocks traded on the Shanghai Stock Exchange, one of the largest stock exchanges in China. It represents a broad spectrum of the Chinese economy including state-owned enterprises, financial institutions, and emerging tech companies. The index is a key indicator for investors monitoring China’s economic growth, market sentiment, and the performance of major sectors such as manufacturing, finance, and consumer goods. Widely followed both domestically and internationally, the SSEC reflects the dynamic changes in China’s capital markets and government policy impacts.
