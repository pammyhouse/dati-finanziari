Dati finanziari per: Alibaba Group Holding Limited (BABA)

Alibaba Group Holding Limited, through its subsidiaries, provides technology infrastructure and marketing reach to help merchants, brands, retailers, and other businesses to engage with their users and customers worldwide. The company operates in four segments: China Commerce, International Commerce, Local Consumer Services, and Cainiao. It operates Taobao Marketplace, a social commerce platform; Tmall, a third-party online and mobile commerce platform; Alibaba.com, a business-to-business online platform that serves small and medium-sized enterprises; AliExpress, a retail marketplace; and Lazada, an e-commerce platform. The company also offers digital media and entertainment platforms, including Youku, a video streaming platform; and provides cloud computing services, such as elastic computing, database, storage, virtualization, networking, and security. In addition, it offers logistics services; payment and financial services via Ant Group; and innovation initiatives and other services. Alibaba Group Holding Limited was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Hangzhou, the People's Republic of China.
