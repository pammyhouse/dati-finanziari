Dati finanziari per: Allianz SE (ALV.DE)

Allianz SE is a German multinational financial services company and one of the world's leading insurers and asset managers. The group operates through four main business segments: Property-Casualty insurance, Life/Health insurance, Asset Management, and Corporate and Other. Through its primary asset management brands, PIMCO and Allianz Global Investors, the company manages trillions of euros in third-party assets. Allianz is a systemic pillar of the global financial system, focusing on integrated risk management and the expansion of digital insurance platforms. The company is committed to a net-zero investment portfolio by 2050. Its headquarters are in Munich, Germany.
