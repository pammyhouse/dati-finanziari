Dati finanziari per: Fidelity Nasdaq Composite Index Tracking Stock ETF (ONEQ)

ONEQ is the Fidelity Nasdaq Composite Index Tracking Stock ETF, designed to provide comprehensive exposure to the Nasdaq Composite Index. Unlike funds that track only the largest companies, ONEQ aims to reflect the performance of almost all domestic and international common stocks listed on the Nasdaq Stock Market. This creates a diversified portfolio with a significant tilt toward technology, biotechnology, and consumer discretionary sectors, capturing the growth of both established industry giants and innovative, smaller-cap firms. The fund serves as a powerful tool for investors seeking broad representation of the high-growth segments of the U.S. economy. Its performance is highly sensitive to trends in digital transformation, interest rate cycles—which disproportionately affect growth stocks—consumer sentiment, and the overall innovation landscape in the global technology sector.
