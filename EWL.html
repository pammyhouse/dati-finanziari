Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI Switzerland ETF (EWL)

EWL is the iShares MSCI Switzerland ETF, which seeks to track the investment results of an index composed of large- and mid-cap Swiss equities. The fund offers exposure to one of the most stable and defensive economies in the world, covering critical sectors such as healthcare, consumer staples, and financials. By investing in EWL, participants gain access to some of the world's most significant multinational corporations listed on the SIX Swiss Exchange. The ETF's performance is driven by the strength of the Swiss Franc, interest rate policies set by the Swiss National Bank, global demand for Swiss pharmaceutical and luxury products, and the overall defensive posture of the Swiss equity market during periods of international volatility. It is a preferred instrument for investors seeking capital preservation and exposure to high-quality, dividend-paying companies within a highly resilient economic framework.
