Dati finanziari per: Airbus SE (AIR.PA)

Airbus SE is a European multinational aerospace corporation and the world's largest manufacturer of commercial airliners. The company is structured into three primary divisions: Airbus (Commercial Aircraft), leading the narrow-body market with the A220 and A320neo families; Airbus Helicopters, the global leader in civil and military helicopter solutions; and Airbus Defence and Space, which manages critical European security projects, orbital systems, and intelligence services. A symbol of cross-border industrial integration, Airbus operates major production facilities in France, Germany, Spain, and the UK. The group is at the forefront of sustainable aviation with its ZEROe project, aimed at pioneering hydrogen-powered commercial flight by 2035. Its main headquarters are situated in Blagnac, France.
