Dati finanziari per: SAP SE (SAP.DE)

SAP SE is a German multinational software corporation and the world's leading provider of enterprise resource planning (ERP) software. The company develops software solutions for managing business operations and customer relations, serving over 400,000 customers in 180 countries. Its product portfolio is centered around SAP S/4HANA, a next-generation ERP suite that utilizes in-memory computing to process vast amounts of data in real-time. In recent years, SAP has undergone a major strategic pivot toward cloud computing, offering its Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP) and various "As-a-Service" models. As a dominant player in the European technology sector, it is focusing on integrating generative AI into its business applications to automate complex corporate workflows. The company is headquartered in Walldorf, Germany.
