Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI Germany ETF (EWG)

EWG is the iShares MSCI Germany ETF, which tracks an index of large- and mid-cap companies in Germany. The fund offers exposure to the core sectors of the German economy, including industrials, consumer discretionary, and financials, reflecting the output of some of the world's most renowned automotive, manufacturing, and chemical corporations. By investing in EWG, market participants gain access to equities listed on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange. The ETF's performance is closely correlated with Germany’s industrial production metrics, domestic economic health, shifts in European monetary policy, and global export demand for high-end capital goods. It serves as a primary vehicle for investors seeking to capture the performance of Europe’s largest economy and its industrial stability.
