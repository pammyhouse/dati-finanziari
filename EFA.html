Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI EAFE ETF (EFA)

EFA represents the iShares MSCI EAFE ETF, managed by BlackRock, which tracks the MSCI EAFE Index, providing exposure to large- and mid-cap equities across developed markets outside the United States and Canada, including Europe, Australasia, and the Far East. The fund is widely used for international diversification and provides investors access to established global economies. Its performance is influenced by international economic growth, currency fluctuations, interest rate differentials, and geopolitical developments across developed markets.
