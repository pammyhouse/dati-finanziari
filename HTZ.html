Dati finanziari per: Hertz Global Holdings, Inc. (HTZ)

Hertz Global Holdings, Inc., through its subsidiaries, provides airport and off-airport vehicle rental and leasing services under the Hertz, Dollar, and Thrifty brands in the United States and internationally. The company offers a range of vehicles, including electric vehicles, luxury and premium vehicles, SUVs, and trucks to individual customers, businesses, governments, and other organizations. In addition to rental services, it provides vehicle leasing and fleet management services. Hertz also sells vehicles through its retail network and online channels. The company was founded in 1918 and is headquartered in Estero, Florida.
