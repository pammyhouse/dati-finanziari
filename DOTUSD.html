Dati finanziari per: Polkadot (DOTUSD)

Polkadot (DOT) is a multi-chain interoperability protocol that connects different specialized blockchains into a single, unified network. Often referred to as a "layer 0" blockchain, it allows diverse chains to transfer data and assets cross-chain, not just tokens. Polkadot achieves this through its unique architecture consisting of a central Relay Chain that provides security and consensus, and multiple Parachains that run in parallel to handle specific applications. This design aims to solve the issues of scalability and fragmentation in the crypto ecosystem. Its native token, DOT, is used for network governance, staking for security, and bonding to connect new parachains to the ecosystem.
