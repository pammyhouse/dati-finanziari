Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI France ETF (EWQ)

EWQ is the iShares MSCI France ETF, designed to provide exposure to a broad range of French companies by tracking the MSCI France Index. The fund covers large- and mid-cap stocks across major sectors, including consumer discretionary, financials, healthcare, and industrials, reflecting the diverse landscape of the French economy. EWQ acts as a primary tool for investors seeking to capture the performance of some of Europe's largest multinational corporations listed on the Euronext Paris exchange. Its performance is heavily influenced by domestic economic data, European Central Bank monetary policy, political stability within the Eurozone, and global demand for French luxury goods and industrial products. It serves as an essential component for portfolios aiming to achieve targeted geographic exposure to one of the most prominent economies in the European Union.
