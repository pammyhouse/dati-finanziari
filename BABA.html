Dati finanziari per: Alibaba Group Holding Limited (BABA)

Alibaba Group Holding Limited, through its subsidiaries, provides technology infrastructure and marketing reach to help merchants, brands, retailers, and other businesses engage with their users and customers worldwide. It operates through seven segments: China Commerce, International Commerce, Local Consumer Services, Cainiao, Cloud, Digital Media and Entertainment, and Innovation Initiatives and Others. The company offers Taobao, a social commerce platform; Tmall for third-party brands and retailers; Alimama, a monetization platform; Alibaba.com for global wholesale; and Lazada and Trendyol for international retail. It also provides grocery, fresh goods, and meal delivery through Freshippo, Ele.me, and other local services. In addition, it offers cloud services, including elastic computing, storage, database, and big data analysis, as well as machine learning and AI services under the Alibaba Cloud brand. Further, the company engages in digital media, entertainment, logistics, and innovation initiatives. Alibaba Group Holding Limited was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Hangzhou, China.
