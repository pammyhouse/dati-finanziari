Dati finanziari per: Deutsche Telekom AG (DTE.DE)

Deutsche Telekom AG is a German multinational telecommunications company and the largest telecommunications provider in Europe by revenue. The company operates several subsidiaries, most notably T-Mobile in the United States, which has become its primary growth driver. Deutsche Telekom provides a wide range of services including fixed-network broadband, mobile communications, Internet, and IPTV for consumers, as well as information and communication technology (ICT) solutions for business and corporate customers. The group is currently leading the 5G rollout across Europe and focusing on fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) expansion and digital cloud services for the enterprise sector. Its headquarters are located in Bonn, Germany.
