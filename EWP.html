Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI Spain ETF (EWP)

EWP is the iShares MSCI Spain ETF, an investment vehicle that tracks the MSCI Spain Index, composed of large- and mid-cap Spanish equities. The fund provides targeted exposure to the Spanish economy, with significant concentrations in sectors such as financials, utilities, and telecommunications. By investing in EWP, participants gain access to companies listed on the Bolsa de Madrid, including some of the largest multinational firms operating in Europe and Latin America. The ETF's performance is driven by Spain's domestic GDP growth, labor market trends, European Central Bank monetary policy, and the regulatory environment within the Eurozone. It serves as an essential tool for investors seeking to capture the economic recovery and industrial performance of one of Southern Europe's largest economies.
