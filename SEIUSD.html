Sei is a specialized Layer 1 blockchain optimized for trading and digital asset exchange, offering a unique infrastructure designed to provide high throughput and sub-second finality. Built using the Cosmos SDK, Sei features a native order matching engine and a Twin-Turbo consensus mechanism that reduces latency and improves efficiency compared to general-purpose blockchains. In 2026, the network has evolved with its V2 parallelized EVM upgrade, combining the best of Ethereum's development ecosystem with Sei's high-speed execution capabilities. The native SEI token is used to pay for transaction fees, participate in network governance, and secure the blockchain through staking, positioning it as a fundamental infrastructure for decentralized finance, gaming, and NFT marketplaces that require near-instant transaction processing.
