Dati finanziari per: Hang Seng Index (HKG50)

The Hang Seng Index (HKG50) is the primary benchmark for the Hong Kong stock market, representing the largest and most liquid companies listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. It covers a diverse range of industries including finance, utilities, property, and technology sectors. The HSI is widely regarded as a barometer of the economic health and investor sentiment in Hong Kong and Greater China. It reflects the performance of blue-chip stocks and is closely watched by both local and international investors, providing insights into market trends, geopolitical influences, and regional economic developments.
