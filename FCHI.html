Dati finanziari per: CAC 40 Index (FCHI)

The CAC 40 Index (FCHI) is the benchmark French stock market index representing the 40 largest and most actively traded companies listed on Euronext Paris. It covers a wide range of sectors including luxury goods, banking, energy, and telecommunications, with major constituents such as LVMH, TotalEnergies, BNP Paribas, and Airbus. The index is capitalization-weighted and reflects the overall health and investor sentiment toward the French economy and European markets. It is frequently used by investors globally as a barometer for economic activity in France and serves as a crucial gauge of market trends in the Eurozone. The CAC 40 is updated continuously during trading sessions and is highly sensitive to geopolitical events, economic data releases, and monetary policy decisions.
