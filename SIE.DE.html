Dati finanziari per: Siemens AG (SIE.DE)

Siemens AG is a German multinational conglomerate and the largest industrial manufacturing company in Europe. The corporation operates through several specialized segments: Digital Industries, focusing on factory automation and digitalization; Smart Infrastructure, providing solutions for energy systems and building technologies; and Mobility, managing rail transport systems. Siemens is a pioneer in the "Industrial Metaverse" and is heavily investing in AI-driven predictive maintenance and additive manufacturing. Through its majority stake in Siemens Healthineers, the group also maintains a dominant position in medical diagnostic equipment. The company is headquartered in Munich and Berlin, Germany.
