Dati finanziari per: Toncoin (TONUSD)

Toncoin (TON) is the native cryptocurrency of The Open Network, a decentralized layer-1 blockchain originally developed by Telegram. Designed for high-speed transactions and massive scalability, TON utilizes a unique multi-level sharding architecture that allows it to process millions of transactions per second. The ecosystem is deeply integrated with the Telegram messaging platform, enabling users to access wallets, make payments, and interact with decentralized applications (dApps) directly within the chat interface. Toncoin is used for transaction fees, securing the network through a proof-of-stake (PoS) consensus mechanism, and as a primary medium for governance and decentralized storage services.
