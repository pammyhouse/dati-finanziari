Dati finanziari per: SPDR Dow Jones Industrial Average ETF Trust (DIA)

DIA tracks the performance of the Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA), an iconic index consisting of 30 prominent blue-chip companies listed on U.S. stock exchanges. Managed by State Street Global Advisors, the fund utilizes a price-weighted methodology, distinguishing it from market-cap-weighted indices. By providing exposure to mature, industry-leading corporations across diverse sectors—excluding transportation and utilities—the ETF serves as a barometer for the broader U.S. industrial economy. Investors often utilize DIA as a core portfolio component for long-term capital appreciation and dividend income, as well as a reliable tool for tactical positioning during periods of market volatility. Its performance is deeply sensitive to corporate governance updates, industrial output data, and shifts in global trade policy, making it a critical asset for investors tracking the stability of established U.S. corporate giants.
