The Swiss Market Index (SWI20) is the benchmark stock market index for Switzerland, comprising the 20 largest and most liquid stocks traded on the SIX Swiss Exchange. The index is considered a blue-chip representation of the Swiss equity market, covering key sectors such as financials, pharmaceuticals, consumer goods, and industrials. It serves as a barometer for the Swiss economy and is widely followed by investors for insights into the country's market trends and economic health.
