Dati finanziari per: Mercedes-Benz Group AG (MBG.DE)

Mercedes-Benz Group AG is a German multinational automotive corporation specializing in premium and luxury vehicles. Following the spin-off of its truck division, the company has narrowed its focus on high-margin passenger cars and vans under the brands Mercedes-Benz, Mercedes-AMG, Mercedes-Maybach, and Mercedes-EQ. The group is executing an "Electric Only" strategy, aiming to go fully electric by the end of the decade where market conditions allow. Mercedes-Benz is a global leader in automotive innovation, particularly in Level 3 autonomous driving systems and advanced in-car software (MB.OS). The company maintains a strong commitment to decarbonization and luxury experiential retail. Its headquarters are in Stuttgart, Germany.
