Dati finanziari per: Invesco QQQ Trust (QQQ)

QQQ represents the Invesco QQQ Trust, an exchange-traded fund designed to track the performance of the Nasdaq-100 Index, which includes 100 of the largest non-financial companies listed on the Nasdaq Stock Market. The fund provides significant exposure to technology and growth-oriented sectors such as information technology, communication services, and consumer discretionary. Managed by Invesco, QQQ follows a passive investment strategy using physical replication to mirror the index’s performance. It is widely utilized by investors seeking growth opportunities, sector concentration in innovative industries, and portfolio diversification. Due to its high liquidity and strong correlation with the technology sector, QQQ is also frequently used for hedging and tactical trading. The ETF’s performance is influenced by technological innovation, corporate earnings, interest rate expectations, and broader macroeconomic trends affecting growth stocks.
