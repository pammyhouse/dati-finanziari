Dati finanziari per: Vanguard S&P 500 ETF (VOO)

VOO represents the Vanguard S&P 500 ETF, an exchange-traded fund that tracks the performance of the S&P 500 Index, which is composed of 500 of the largest U.S. publicly traded companies. This ETF provides broad exposure to the U.S. equity market across all major sectors, including technology, healthcare, financials, and consumer staples. Managed by Vanguard, VOO is renowned for its extremely low expense ratio and efficient tax management, following a passive indexing strategy through full physical replication. It is a cornerstone for many diversified portfolios, favored by long-term investors seeking consistent growth and core market exposure. VOO’s performance serves as a primary benchmark for the overall health of the U.S. economy. Its price movements are driven by corporate profitability, macroeconomic indicators, and shifts in monetary policy from the Federal Reserve.
