Dati finanziari per: Iberdrola, S.A. (IBE.MC)

Iberdrola, S.A. is a Spanish multinational electric utility company and the world's leading producer of wind power. The company operates in four main geographic areas: Spain, the UK (through ScottishPower), the US (through Avangrid), and Brazil (through Neoenergia). Iberdrola is a global benchmark in the energy transition, having invested billions in renewable energy, smart grids, and energy storage systems. Its business model is centered on the electrification of the economy and decarbonization, with a goal of becoming carbon neutral in Europe by 2030. The group is also a pioneer in green hydrogen development for industrial use. Its headquarters are in Bilbao, Spain.
