Dati finanziari per: Enel S.p.A. (ENEL.MI)

Enel S.p.A. is a multinational energy company and one of the world’s leading electricity and gas providers. Founded in 1962 and headquartered in Rome, Enel operates in over 30 countries, focusing on electricity generation, distribution, and renewable energy solutions. It is publicly traded on the Borsa Italiana under the ticker ENEL.MI and is a key player in Italy’s energy transition towards sustainable and low-carbon sources.
