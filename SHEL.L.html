Dati finanziari per: Shell plc (SHEL.L)

Shell plc is a British multinational oil and gas company and one of the world's "supermajors." Its operations encompass the entire energy value chain, including exploration, production, refining, and marketing of oil and natural gas, as well as the manufacturing of chemicals. Shell is currently the global leader in Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) and is aggressively transitioning its business model toward low-carbon energy solutions, such as hydrogen, biofuels, and electric vehicle charging infrastructure. The company aims to become a net-zero emissions energy business by 2050 while maintaining its position as a dividend-stable pillar for institutional investors. Its corporate headquarters are located in London, United Kingdom.
