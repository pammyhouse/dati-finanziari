Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI India ETF (INDY)

INDY is the iShares MSCI India ETF, an investment fund that seeks to track the performance of an index composed of large- and mid-cap Indian companies. By providing exposure to one of the world's fastest-growing major economies, the fund encompasses key sectors such as financials, information technology, and energy. Investing in INDY allows market participants to tap into the growth potential of India's corporate sector, featuring many of the country's most prominent industrial and technological leaders. The ETF's performance is driven by India's robust domestic consumption, infrastructure spending, government fiscal reforms, and its role as a global technology services hub. It remains a primary instrument for investors seeking to capture the long-term structural growth of the Indian market within their international equity allocation.
