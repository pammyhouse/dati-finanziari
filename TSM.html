Dati finanziari per: Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company Limited (TSM)

Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company Limited (TSMC) is a Taiwanese multinational semiconductor contract manufacturing and design company, recognized as the world's most valuable semiconductor firm and the largest dedicated independent foundry. Founded in 1987 by Morris Chang, TSMC pioneered the "pure-play" foundry business model, meaning it solely manufactures silicon wafers for other companies and does not design or market its own branded chips. This neutral stance has allowed TSMC to partner with the world's leading technology companies, including Apple, Nvidia, Advanced Micro Devices (AMD), and Qualcomm. TSMC dominates the leading-edge chip manufacturing market, consistently being the first to reach high-volume production on advanced nodes like 5-nanometer and 3-nanometer processes. Due to the critical nature of its technology for everything from smartphones to artificial intelligence accelerators and military hardware, TSMC holds immense geopolitical significance, prompting recent strategic expansions of its fabrication facilities into the United States, Japan, and Europe.
