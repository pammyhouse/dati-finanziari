Dati finanziari per: Rio Tinto Group (RIO.L)

Rio Tinto Group is a leading global mining and metals corporation that focuses on finding, mining, and processing Earth's mineral resources. The company's primary products include iron ore, aluminium, copper, lithium, and industrial minerals. As a major supplier of iron ore to China, Rio Tinto is a key indicator of global industrial demand. The group is increasingly pivoting toward "materials for the energy transition," investing heavily in high-grade iron ore projects like Simandou and expanding its lithium production capacity. Rio Tinto is also a pioneer in autonomous mining technology, operating the world's largest fleet of driverless trucks. Its dual-listed corporate headquarters are in London, United Kingdom, and Melbourne, Australia.
