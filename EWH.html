Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI Hong Kong ETF (EWH)

EWH is the iShares MSCI Hong Kong ETF, which seeks to track the investment results of an index composed of large- and mid-cap companies in Hong Kong. The fund provides direct exposure to one of the world's most significant financial hubs, spanning sectors such as financials, real estate, and consumer services. By investing in EWH, participants gain access to companies listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, which serves as a critical gateway for international capital entering the Asian markets. The ETF's performance is heavily influenced by the macroeconomic policies of Hong Kong, fluctuations in property market valuations, regulatory developments in the region, and the broader economic relationship between Hong Kong and mainland China. It remains a key tool for investors looking to balance their portfolios with exposure to highly developed and strategic Asian markets.
