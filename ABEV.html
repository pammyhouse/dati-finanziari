Dati finanziari per: Ambev S.A. (ABEV)

Ambev S.A. is a Brazilian brewing company and a subsidiary of Anheuser-Busch InBev. It is the largest brewery in Latin America and the fourth largest in the world, producing a wide variety of beers, carbonated soft drinks, and other non-alcoholic beverages. Its powerful brand portfolio includes Skol, Brahma, Antarctica, and Quilmes, alongside the distribution of Budweiser and Pepsi products in South America. Ambev operates an extensive logistics and distribution network that serves millions of points of sale across 18 countries.
