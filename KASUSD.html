Dati finanziari per: Kaspa (KASUSD)

Kaspa is a decentralized Layer-1 blockchain that utilizes the revolutionary BlockDAG (Directed Acyclic Graph) architecture to achieve extreme scalability and near-instant transaction finality. Based on the GHOSTDAG protocol—a scalable generalization of Nakamoto Consensus—Kaspa allows parallel blocks to coexist and be ordered in consensus, effectively solving the traditional blockchain trilemma by maintaining high throughput without compromising security or decentralization. In 2026, Kaspa has solidified its position as a leading Proof-of-Work (PoW) network, processing up to 10 blocks per second. Its fair launch, with no pre-mine or venture capital allocation, ensures a highly decentralized distribution. The native KAS token is used to reward miners, pay for transaction fees, and power the ecosystem's expanding smart contract capabilities via the KRC-20 standard.
