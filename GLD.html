Dati finanziari per: SPDR Gold Shares (GLD)

GLD represents the SPDR Gold Shares ETF, one of the largest physically backed gold ETFs in the world, designed to track the price of gold bullion. Managed by State Street Global Advisors, the fund holds physical gold in secure vaults and offers investors exposure to gold without the need for direct ownership. GLD is widely used as a hedge against inflation, currency depreciation, and geopolitical risk, and is a key component of diversified portfolios.
