Dati finanziari per: Ferrari N.V. (RACE.MI)

Ferrari N.V. is an iconic Italian luxury sports car manufacturer based in Maranello, Italy, globally recognized as a symbol of exclusivity, extreme performance, and automotive excellence. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 out of the Alfa Romeo racing division, the company built its first car in 1940 and has since become inextricably linked with motorsport, most notably as the oldest and most successful team in Formula One history (Scuderia Ferrari). Ferrari's business model is built on artificial scarcity; by strictly limiting production numbers, the brand maintains immense pricing power and unrivaled brand equity. The company produces a range of V8 and V12 engine sports cars, grand tourers, and ultra-exclusive limited-edition "Icona" series models. Recently, Ferrari successfully expanded into the luxury SUV market with the Purosangue and has embarked on a strategic transition toward electrification, introducing plug-in hybrids like the SF90 Stradale, with its first fully electric supercar slated for the near future.
