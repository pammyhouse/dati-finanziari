Dati finanziari per: iShares MSCI Italy ETF (EWI)

EWI is the iShares MSCI Italy ETF, which tracks an index of large- and mid-cap companies across various sectors of the Italian equity market. The fund provides targeted exposure to Italy's economy, encompassing key industries such as financials, energy, and utilities. By investing in EWI, market participants gain a diversified approach to Italian equities, allowing them to capitalize on the performance of companies listed on the Borsa Italiana. The ETF's performance is closely tied to Italy's macroeconomic environment, including GDP growth, government fiscal policies, European Central Bank interest rate decisions, and overall investor sentiment toward the Eurozone's third-largest economy. It is a primary instrument for investors looking to integrate specific geographic allocation to the Italian market into their broader international investment strategy.
