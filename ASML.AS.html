Dati finanziari per: ASML Holding N.V. (ASML.AS)

ASML Holding N.V. is a Dutch multinational corporation and the world's premier supplier of photolithography systems for the semiconductor industry. Headquartered in Veldhoven, Netherlands, ASML holds a virtual monopoly on Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) lithography machines, the most complex and expensive manufacturing equipment ever built. These machines use incredibly short wavelengths of light to print microscopic circuits onto silicon wafers, making ASML the critical enabler of Moore's Law and the production of the world's most advanced microchips (such as those powering modern smartphones, AI data centers, and advanced military tech). The company's customer base includes all major chip foundries, notably TSMC, Intel, and Samsung. Due to its irreplaceable position at the absolute chokepoint of the global semiconductor supply chain, ASML has become a company of immense geopolitical importance, heavily impacted by international trade regulations and export controls regarding advanced technology transfers.
