Dati finanziari per: Stellantis N.V. (STLAM.MI)

Stellantis N.V. is a multinational automotive company formed in 2021 from the merger of Fiat Chrysler Automobiles (FCA) and PSA Group. Headquartered in Amsterdam, Stellantis designs, manufactures, and sells vehicles under brands such as Jeep, Peugeot, Fiat, Citroën, Opel, and Alfa Romeo. It is publicly traded on the Borsa Italiana under the ticker STLAM.MI and is one of the world’s largest car manufacturers by volume.
